About Me

Hi, I’m the creator of WiseScroll. I built this project because I believe scientific knowledge should feel more open, modern, and accessible to everyone — not only to researchers and academics.

My goal is to make discovering research papers feel as natural and engaging as scrolling through your favorite apps, while still keeping the depth and value of real science.

Why I started WiseScroll

I noticed that research is often hard to approach for ordinary people. Even when someone is curious, academic papers can feel too dense, too technical, and too difficult to browse casually. WiseScroll began as an idea to make research discovery more intuitive and less intimidating.

What inspires me

I am inspired by education, technology, curiosity, and the idea that complex knowledge can be presented in a way that feels elegant and understandable. I want to build tools that help people learn faster and explore deeper.
