What is WiseScroll?

WiseScroll is designed for students, curious readers, researchers, and anyone who wants a better way to discover scientific knowledge.

Browse papers like a feed

Instead of searching through endless academic websites, users can scroll through papers in a clean and intuitive interface.

Find relevant research faster

WiseScroll helps users quickly understand what a paper is about and decide whether they want to open the full version.

Built for normal people too

This is not only for scientists. It is also for people who are curious about science but do not want to read difficult academic language all the time.

Why people would use it

Reading papers is often slow, confusing, and frustrating. WiseScroll makes discovery easier by turning research into a more accessible experience.

  • Easy paper discovery
  • Cleaner reading experience
  • Less time wasted on irrelevant articles
  • Better access to scientific knowledge
